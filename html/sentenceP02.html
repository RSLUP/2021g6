Sentence Pattern 02

Subject + Intransitive Verb + Adverbial (S+V+A)

  1. John laughed heartily.
  2. She was crying bitterly, in the bathroom, last night.
  3. Rumours spread fast.
  4. Birds fly in the sky.
  5. Fire burns, impartially.
  6. The moon is shining over the forest.
  7. The baby is sleeping, conveniently.
  8. Kumari was singing softly, in the party, last evening.
  9. The bell has rung intermittently.
  10. The sun rose over the mountain.
  11. Dolphins leap competitively.
  12. Dogs bark meaninglessly.
