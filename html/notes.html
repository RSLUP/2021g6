Personal Notes

Often in our daily life we need to write short notes to people. We may wish to request something from someone, thank a friend, apologise, make excuses etc.
This Personal Notes are;

  • don't begin with Dear......
  • are brief and to the point
  • are written to people we know very well
  • don't end with 'Yours sincerely/ faithfully'
  • are use contracted forms or contractions
    Eg-:Don't wait for me

Note of request

Nishantha,

Please don't lock the door. I'll be late. I'm going to watch a movie with Piyal. Be back at 11.

Sam

Note of Thanks

Nirupama,

Thanks for sending me your Science notes. I received it yeaterday.

Sita

Note of Apology

Wimal,

I'm sorry. I can't come for the match. Have to go to kandy urgently.

Samitha
