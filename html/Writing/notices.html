Notices

Notices are different from memos and notes, because they are usually put up on Notice Boards or passed around for every one in the office read. They are not personal or confidential or addressed to a selected few. Notices are written to announce an event or give information, which will benifit everyone working in the organization. Notices are not limited to offices: schools, tutories and other institutions too, use Notices to make announcements and give information.


Office & Company Ltd.
550, Vauxhall Street, Colombo 10.
NOTICE

All employees of office & Company Ltd are entitled to followEnglish classes, which will be conducted on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4.00 to 5.00 p.m

The management of office & Company Ltd. will pay the fees for the course. The duration of the English course is from Tue. 07 August to Th. 25 October, 2001.

The classe will be conducted in the Boardroom of our office, and will be limited to 25 participants.

If you're interested, please give your name to the Secretary, Human Resource Division by 12.00 noon, Th. 31 July, 2001.


..................................
Manager, Human Resources
